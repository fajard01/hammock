Tales From the Far Side

six months in Peru

Hello!
Welcome to Peru!!

You find us halfway around the world
in a completely new and unknown land,
full of places to find,
people to meet and adventures to be had.

There will be hiccups - injuries and sunburns,
thunderstorms and missed connections.

But there is only real adventure ahead.



So come explore this incredible land,
from coast
to desert
to mountain
to jungle.

From the start of civilisation
through incans
and spaniards
to the modern peru,
full of colour and music and
damn good food.

Hope you enjoy!
